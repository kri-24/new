What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. It encompasses a wide range of technologies, including machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics.

AI has applications in various fields such as healthcare, finance, transportation, and entertainment. For example, AI powers recommendation systems on platforms like Netflix and helps in medical diagnostics.

Key types of AI include:

  • Narrow AI: Designed for specific tasks, like voice assistants (e.g., Siri).
  • General AI: Hypothetical AI with human-like intelligence across domains.
  • Superintelligent AI: AI that surpasses human intelligence, a topic of ongoing debate.

AI is evolving rapidly, with advancements in deep learning and neural networks driving innovations. However, it also raises ethical concerns like privacy, bias, and job displacement.
